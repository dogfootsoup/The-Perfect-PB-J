The Perfect PB & J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favourite sandiwch. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Any peanut butter and jam will work but my recommendation is to use grape jelly and all natural peanut butter.


ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of all natural peanut butter

Directions

  • Step 1: cut the crust from both slices of bread.
  • Step 2: take a butter knife and evenly spread peanut butter on one of your slices of bread.
  • Step 3: take a spoon and evenly spread grape jelly on your other slice of bread.
  • Step 4: join the two slices of bread with the jelly and peanut butter sides facing each other.
